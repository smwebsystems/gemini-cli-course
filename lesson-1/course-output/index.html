About Us

Founded in 2010, Pets Delight Animal Shelter is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. We believe that every pet is a family member waiting to happen.

Our dedicated team of volunteers and veterinary professionals work tirelessly to ensure that every animal that enters our doors receives the highest quality care and the best possible chance at finding their forever home.

What We Need

Monetary Donations

Funds for medical surgeries, vaccinations, and daily operations are our most critical need.

Supplies & Food

High-quality pet food, clean blankets, toys, and cleaning supplies help keep our residents happy.

Volunteer Time

From walking dogs to cleaning kennels, your time is a valuable gift to our animals.

Your generosity directly impacts the lives of hundreds of animals each year.

Who Do We Serve

We serve the abandoned, the neglected, and the lost animals of our local community. While dogs and cats make up the majority of our residents, we open our doors to all domestic animals in crisis.

  • Stray and abandoned domestic pets
  • Animals from overcrowded high-kill shelters
  • Pets of owners in sudden financial crisis
  • Injured wildlife (stabilization and transfer)
